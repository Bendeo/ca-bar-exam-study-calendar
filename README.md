### Daily Study Plan
Each week is broken down into daily tasks including:

### Interactive Features

#### Week Expansion/Collapse
- Click on any week header to expand or collapse the detailed schedule
- Use the "Expand All Weeks" button to view all weeks at once

#### Progress Tracking
Track your study progress with built-in counters:
- **MBE Questions**: Goal of 2000+ practice questions
- **Essays**: Goal of 40+ practice essays
- **Performance Tests**: Goal of 10+ PTs

Click the buttons to update your progress as you complete practice:
- "+10 MBE" - Add 10 MBE questions to your count
- "+1 Essay" - Add 1 essay to your count
- "+1 PT" - Add 1 performance test to your count

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser!

#### Print Calendar
Use the "Print Calendar" button to create a physical copy of your study schedule.

### Customization Tips

1. **Adjust for Your Schedule**: The calendar is a guide. Feel free to modify based on your strengths and weaknesses.

2. **Set Your Exam Date**: Edit the `script.js` file to set your actual exam date and enable the countdown timer.

3. **Track Your Weak Areas**: During Week 4-5, focus extra time on subjects where you're scoring below 60%.

4. **Rest Days**: The calendar includes lighter days on Sundays. Don't skip rest - it's crucial for retention!
