# California Bar Exam Study Calendar

**Study Period:** December 27, 2025 - February 23, 2026 (9 Weeks)  
**Exam Dates:** February 24-25, 2026

## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive 9-week study schedule for the California Bar Exam. The schedule includes approximately 494 hours of study time covering all MBE subjects, California essay subjects, and performance tests.

## Subjects Covered

### MBE Subjects (Multistate):
- Constitutional Law
- Contracts & Sales
- Criminal Law & Procedure
- Evidence
- Real Property
- Torts
- Civil Procedure

### California Essay Subjects:
- Business Associations (Corporations, Agency, Partnerships)
- Community Property
- Professional Responsibility
- Remedies
- Wills & Trusts

### Performance Tests:
- Multiple practice PTs throughout
- Graded PTs with review sessions
- Simulated exam conditions

## How to Use This Calendar

Each day is organized into two study blocks:
- **Morning** - Primary study sessions
- **Afternoon (1)** - Additional practice and review

Tasks include lectures, practice questions, essays, and review sessions with time estimates for each task.

## Study Tips

- Take 10-15 minute breaks between study sessions
- Review mistakes immediately after practice questions
- Get 7-8 hours of sleep each night
- Stay hydrated and maintain healthy eating habits
- Use active recall and spaced repetition techniques
